About Hockeylytics

         We look to provide an in-depth website for our users that is a step above other data visualization websites on the market. Through our website, coaches and players will have:
  • A secure login feature
  • The ability for coaches to enter in team, player, match, and player performance data
  • A detailed dashboard with custom filtering options to see both graphs and tables of their data
  • The ability for coaches to grant their players access to their data
