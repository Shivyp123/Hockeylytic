Coach

How does this site work?

This site allows coaches to enter in data related to their hockey team, view visualizations about it, and give players access to their data. You can enter in the following stats:
  • Teams
  • Players on that team
  • Matches
  • Player Stats

Do my players need to create an account for me to enter in data for them?

No. You can enter in stats for your players without them having an account. If they would like to create a player account and see their data, simply use the invite player tab to give them access to their personal dashboard
Player

I just made a player account, and want to see my stats. What do I do?

To see your stats, first copy your unique ID under your username in Account Settings -> Profile. Send this to your coach. He will now be able to link your account to a player he created in the invite player tab.

Why can't I compare my team's stats to another?

You can only compare two teams that you are a member of. Otherwise you would be able to see the stats of every team on the site, which would be bad.

Why don't I see any data in season stats / team comparison?

Please give your unique ID to your coach to gain access to your stats. If you don't see any data, your coach may not have entered in any data for you yet.
General

Why should I use your site?

There are a number of reasons why you should use our website:
  • We have a good visualization of player's personal performance stats. And there will be more options in the future updates.
  • Make your team in a blink of an eye with your collected data.
  • It's FREE and fun to use.

Is this website free or subscription based?

It's free for everyone who is interested in hockey team management and loves to see and compare self performance stats with others.

Will this website be maintained?

We plan to add more robust and detailed graphs in the future, and adding more security layers to our site. It will likely stay on the web until Fall 2021.
